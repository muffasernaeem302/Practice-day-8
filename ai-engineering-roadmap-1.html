⚡ Your Questions Answered First

Is AI Engineering the right choice? Yes — one of the strongest choices you can make from Pakistan right now. Demand is exploding globally and companies actively hire remote engineers from anywhere.

Can you get a job remotely from Pakistan? Absolutely. Platforms like Upwork, Toptal, and direct hiring from US/EU startups are very active. Pakistani engineers already do this in large numbers.

Can that job lead to a visa abroad? Yes. Once you have 1–2 years of AI experience, you can apply for Germany's Skilled Worker Visa, Canada's Global Talent Stream, or UK Global Talent visa. Companies that hire you remotely often sponsor relocation too.

About "AI for Everyone" by Andrew Ng: Good awareness course but it is not a technical course. It will not get you a job. It is good to understand AI business context, but you need the technical specializations.

📌 Coursera vs YouTube — Your Strategy: Use Coursera for certifications that go on your CV (especially deeplearning.ai and IBM courses — companies recognize these). Use YouTube for concepts you need explained differently, quick tutorials, and project walkthroughs. Both together is the optimal approach.

Remote Work & Going Abroad
Where Pakistani AI engineers actually get hired and how to get a visa

🌐 Remote Job Platforms

  • Upwork (start here, build reviews)
  • Toptal (harder, but premium rates)
  • Turing.com (vetted, stable clients)
  • LinkedIn (direct company outreach)
  • Remote.co / We Work Remotely
  • AngelList (startups, often flexible)
  • Contra.com (freelance AI projects)

✈️ Visa Pathways After 1–2 Years XP

  • Germany: Skilled Worker Visa (IT)
  • Canada: Global Talent Stream (fast)
  • UK: Global Talent Visa (no job offer needed)
  • Netherlands: Highly Skilled Migrant
  • UAE: Golden Visa (AI professionals)
  • USA: H-1B (needs employer sponsor)
